Orrick, Herrington & Sutcliffe

Trainined at one of the world's leading technology law firms, advising on commercial agreements, digital regulation, and corporate VC & M&A.

At Orrick I sat across the firm's core practice areas, developing a commercially grounded skill set shaped by the demands of high-value, cross-border matters. I drafted commercial contracts, advised on UK and EU data protection matters, and supported complex corporate and financing deals.

Throughout my training I consistently demonstrated a commercial mindset, strong written advocacy, and the ability to take ownership of workstreams, lead client interactions and adapt to fast-moving, technically complex matters. I also led training sessions on document automation tools and utilised AI integrations including HarveyAI and Draftwise.

Commercial & Technology

  • Drafted and reviewed commercial contracts including service, licence and contractor agreements
  • Prepared novation agreements, IP assignments, NDAs, variation and termination deeds, and a business separation transitional service agreement
  • Conducted commercial, IT and data due diligence on acquisitions ranging from $12M to $20M
  • Prepared clear IP and liability clauses for AI voice-oriented systems and SaaS clients

Data Privacy & Cyber

  • Advised on UK/EU GDPR data protection principles including lawful basis of processing and data transfer requirements; drafted DPAs and SCCs
  • Developed and remediated privacy and cookie policies under PECR and the ePrivacy Directive
  • Provided commercially-minded advice for DPOs and DSARs, including on deleted data and exemptions
  • Drafted document preservation memoranda and privilege protocols to mitigate litigation and regulatory risk in response to a breach
  • Triaged and analysed complaints and pre-action correspondence in a large international data breach

Corporate, VC & M&A

  • Drafted transaction and ancillary documents for technology-focused VC investments, including UK to US flips, and mid-market M&A
  • Managed both company and investor-side clients during signing and completion.
  • Led a client call following investigative research on a complex option plan restructuring

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

  • Prepared court applications, witness statements and bundles for commercial disputes and enforcement proceedings
  • Attended court in relation to an employee data breach and participated in strategy discussions balancing legal, commercial and reputational considerations

Banking & Structured Finance

  • Led automation of several hundred documents for a $75M financing package
  • Prepared complex group structure charts and reviewed amendment and restatement agreements for venture debt and financial restructuring matters

Pro Bono & Leadership

  • Managed pro bono initiatives including advocacy on a care application for a severely disabled child
  • Mentored juniors and organised a training programme for the trainee cohort
  • Led training on automation tools and utilised HarveyAI, ChatGPT and Draftwise
